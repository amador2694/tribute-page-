Major Events in Chester Bennington's life
  • 1976: Chester Bennington is born March 20 in Phoenix, Arizona.
  • 1993: Bennington first began singing with a band called Sean Dowdell and His Friends? and together they releasedan eponymous three-track cassette in 1993.
  • 1996: Bennington's son Jaime was born May 12, 1996 from his relationship with Elka Brand.
  • 2000: Linkin Park released their debut album, Hybrid Theory, through Warner Bros. Records.
  • 2003: The band's second album, Meteora(2003), reached number one on the Billboard 200 album chart.
  • 2005: Bennington co-founded Dead by Sunrise, en electronic rock band from Los Angeles, California, with Orgy and Julien-K members Amir Derakh and Ryan Shuck in 2005.
  • 2007: Bennington sustained a wrist injury in October 2007 while attempting to jump off a platform during a show in Melbourne at the Rod Laver Arena.
  • 2009: Dead by Sunrise released their debut album Out of Ashes on October 13, 2009.
  • 2011: Bennington overcame his drug addiction and would go on to denounce drug use in future interviews.
  • 2013: Stone Temple Pilots recruited Bennington after the band parted ways with long-time lead singer Scott Weiland.
  • 2015: Bennington injured his ankle in January 2015 during a basketball game, as a result Linkin Park canceled the remainder of their tour to allow Bennington to recover.
  • 2017: Bennington was found dead by his housekeeper at his home in Palos Verdes Estates, California around 9:00 a.m. PDT on July 20, 2017.
